My Weekly Workout Schedule

Below is my weekly workout schedule. It is always helpful to have a schedule as it can increase adherence. However, it does not mean one should strictly follow it. The joy of exercising involves working out and modifying that schedule to fit your current needs!

Workout Schedule

My Weekly Schedule
Day Target Workouts
Monday Full Body Strength Training: Squats, push-ups, dumbbell rows, planks.
Tuesday Light Cardio: walk on treadmill and/or stairmaster.
Wednesday Upper Body Strength: Shoulder presses, bicep curls, tricep dips.
Thursday Rest or Stretching: Yoga or gentle stretching.
Friday Lower Body Strength: Lunges, leg press, leg extensions, calf raises.
Saturday Cardio & Core: walk on treadmill and/or stairmaster. Ab crunches, planks.
Sunday Rest and Recovery.
The number of hours I workout a day:

I try to workout for 2 hours at most for an efficient session.
