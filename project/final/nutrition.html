Nutrition and Recovery

Nutrition is vital for fueling workouts and helping muscles to recover after a workout. It is also important for fueling your body throughout the day!

5 Nutrition Tips to Maximize Workouts and Fitness

  1. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water before, during, and after workouts to maintain optimal performance and aid recovery.
  2. Balanced Meals: Consume meals that include a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats to fuel your body and support muscle repair.
  3. Pre-Workout Nutrition: Eat a light snack or meal rich in carbohydrates and moderate in protein about 1-2 hours before exercising to provide energy.
  4. Post-Workout Recovery: Within 30-60 minutes after exercise, consume a meal or snack with protein and carbohydrates to replenish glycogen stores and promote muscle recovery.
  5. Avoid Processed Foods: Limit intake of processed and sugary foods that can lead to inflammation and hinder recovery.
