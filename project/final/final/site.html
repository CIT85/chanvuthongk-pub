Site Map

This page shows the overall structure of my website and how each page is organized.

Pages

  • Home – Workout Plan
    • Image and introduction to my fitness journey
    • Health and Fitness – overview of why fitness is important
    • Benefits of Exercising – list of key benefits
    • Different Types of Exercises – descriptions of:
      • Yoga
      • Strength
      • Cardio
      • Stretching/Flexibility
  • Weekly Workout Schedule
    • Image and introduction to why a schedule helps
    • Workout Schedule – table listing days and target workouts
    • Workout Duration – details element showing hours per day
    • Local Gyms – list of Fresno-area gyms with address and phone
  • Nutrition and Recovery
    • Image and introduction to the role of nutrition
    • 5 Nutrition Tips – ordered list of tips for fueling workouts and recovery
    • Rest, Sleep, and Stress Management – page explaining why rest days, sleep, and stress management are important for fitness progress
  • Motivation and Tips
    • Image and introduction to staying motivated
    • Motivation Tips List – unordered list of strategies to stay on track
  • About Me
    • Introduction to who I am and why I started my fitness journey.
    • Image and story describing my background and goals.
    • Reasons for building this website and how it supports my well-being.
